# Contributors

HamClock-Next is a community-driven project dedicated to preserving and modernizing the HamClock experience.

## Original Author & Visionary
*   **Elwood Downey (WB0OEW)**: Architect of the original HamClock. Without his years of hard work, creative design, and dedication to the hobby, this project would not exist.

## Core Maintainers
*   **HamClock Community Maintainers**: Developing the SDL2 reconstruction, cross-platform build systems, and direct-data integration.

## Key Data Providers
We are grateful to the organizations that provide the public data powering this application:
*   **NASA SVS/Blue Marble**: High-resolution world maps and Dial-a-Moon imagery.
*   **NOAA / SWPC**: Solar data, K-Index, and Space Weather forecasting.
*   **PSK Reporter**: Live band activity data.
*   **Open-Meteo**: Global weather forecasting.
*   **NG3K (ADXO)**: DXPedition scheduling.
*   **POTA / SOTA**: Real-time activity feeds.
